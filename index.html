The Mission

First Carolina is a North Carolina social media collective and movement built for young Republicans, activists, and rising conservative voices.

We connect, promote, and sharpen one another. We create real intra-party debate so the strongest ideas and the strongest candidates rise. No gatekeepers. No participation trophies. Only results.

Our purpose is simple: push the best young Republicans forward.
